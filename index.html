our story
BUILT ON
GRACE.

SFCNT Grace started as a conversation between two people 1,000 miles apart — united by faith and a call to serve young adults who felt lost, unseen, or without direction.

We believe grace isn't just a concept — it's a lifestyle. You are a living testimony, unfinished and still being worked on.

what we stand for
01 — Faith

Helping young adults rediscover and anchor their identity in something greater than themselves.

02 — Creativity

Using art, design, and storytelling as tools for healing, growth, and purpose-driven living.

03 — Community

Building a tribe of young adults who carry grace, live with purpose, and lead with love.

04 — Identity

Empowering the next generation to walk in their calling — not perform for it.
